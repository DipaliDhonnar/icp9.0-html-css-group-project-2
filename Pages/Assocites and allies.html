BAJI PRABHU

BAJI PRABHU

Shivaji Maharaj's mother, a significant influence in his early life. Jijabai taught Shivaji the importance of strategy, values, and dharma by telling him stories from the great epics and folklore. She taught him the art of politics and prepared him to be a just and honest ruler. She even supervised his weapon training sessions. Jijabai told young Shivaji the stories of Rama, Krishna, Bhim, and Abhimanyu. After listening to these stories, the thoughts of displaying valour like the brave men came to Shivaji's mind. The thought of fighting against injustice also came to his mind.

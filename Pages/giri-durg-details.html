Pratapgad

Location: Satara, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1656

Significance: Known for the historic Battle of Pratapgad between Shivaji Maharaj and Afzal Khan. The fort is strategically located on a hill, which provides a panoramic view of the surrounding region.

Vasota Fort

Location: Satara, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 12th century (originally built, later modified by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj)

Significance: Located in the Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary, Vasota Fort played a strategic role in the Maratha defense system. It offers stunning views of the Sahyadri range and is known for its challenging trek.

Shivneri Fort

Location: Junnar, Pune District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: The fort was originally built during the 13th century, later modified by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 13th century (originally built, significantly fortified by Shivaji Maharaj in the 17th century)

Significance: Shivneri Fort is famously known as the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. It holds historical importance as a strategic military fort and is renowned for its well-preserved architecture and historical artifacts.

Purandar Fort

Location: Pune District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: The fort was originally built by the Yadavas, later taken over and fortified by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 13th century (originally built, later fortified by Shivaji Maharaj in the 17th century)

Significance: Purandar Fort is known for its historical battles and strategic location. It played a crucial role in Shivaji Maharaj's empire and is notable for its robust fortifications and historical significance in the Maratha defense system.

Raigad Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1656

Significance: The capital of the Maratha Empire under Shivaji Maharaj, known for its strategic importance and impressive fortifications.

Sinhagad Fort

Location: Pune District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Built during the Yadava dynasty, later modified by Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1670

Significance: Known for its role in the Battle of Sinhagad, a significant Maratha victory, and its picturesque location.

Torna Fort

Location: Pune District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1645

Significance: The first fort captured by Shivaji Maharaj, marking the beginning of his campaign to establish a Maratha empire.

Lohagad Fort

Location: Pune District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: The fort's origins are unclear, but it was later fortified by Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 18th century (refurbished)

Significance: Known for its robust fortifications and scenic beauty, it served as a strategic military location.

Rajmachi Fort

Location: Pune District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 17th century

Significance: Comprising two forts, Shrivardhan and Manaranjan, it was crucial for controlling the trade routes between Pune and Mumbai.

Korigad Fort

Location: Pune District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: The fort was initially built in the 15th century by the Koli clan, later taken over by Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 15th century

Significance: Noted for its well-preserved ramparts and panoramic views of the surrounding region.

Salota Fort

Location: Nashik District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: The fort's origins are unclear, but it was later fortified during the Maratha period

Year: 17th century

Significance: Lesser-known but notable for its strategic location and role in local defense.

Kalsubai Fort

Location: Nashik District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: The fort was built in the 18th century

Year: 18th century

Significance: The highest fort in Maharashtra, offering spectacular views from its summit.

Ratangad Fort

Location: Nashik District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 17th century

Significance: Known for its rugged terrain and historical significance in Maratha defense.

Harihar Fort

Location: Nashik District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: The fort's origins are unclear, but it was fortified during the Maratha period

Year: 17th century

Significance: Famous for its unique step-like architecture and challenging trek.

Kothaligad Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: The fort was built in the 18th century

Year: 18th century

Significance: Known for its cave temples and scenic surroundings.
